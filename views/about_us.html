About Fanpickd

Fanpickd, as the name suggests “picked by fans”. Why Fanpickd? During the initial stage, when we were selling our product at a very small scale, we got immense compliments from people for our product. They were genuinely liking our product and many of them advised us to launch our product in the market as a brand. It was then when we thought of stepping out in the market. Looking at the journey of our product we coined the term “Fanpickd”.
